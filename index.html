Xchange Up

Exchange currency at the fair rate

Xchange Up is a P2P currency exchange app that enables users to securely connect with other trusted users at airports in order to exchange foreign currency for the exact fair rate.

Human-to-Human

When you are travelling from Europe to the States and you need to exchange EUR to USD, we match you with trusted users that just landed and need to exchange USD to EUR. The same applies when you just want to exchange the remaining cash at the end of your trip.

Avoid unfair rates

The exchange rate that most ATMs, banks and agencies (including "commision free" ones) apply is up to 15% higher than the real one. With Xchange Up you just pay a 1% of the amount you want to exchange, with a minimum of 1 EUR transaction fee.

Secure transactions

All our users have their identity verified via phone number, credit card and photo, and you can write reviews about your match. In addition, the meeting point for the actual exchange is in the security area of an international airport.
